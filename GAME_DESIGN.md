## ðŸŽ® CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP

### The Roguelike Cycle

1. **HOMEBASE (Asteroid Shelter)**
   - Repair and upgrade ship systems
   - Rebuild fractured AI crew members
   - Manage resources
   - Review collected fragments and clues
   - Plan next expedition

2. **SOLAR SYSTEM SELECTION**
   - Identify systems by their star type
   - Each star emits specific radiation types
   - Match radiation needs for RFE (Reference Frame Engine) fuel
   - Assess risk vs. reward

3. **EXPEDITION (The Run)**
   - Travel to selected system
   - Explore derelict stations, dead worlds, drifting debris
   - Collect resources, data fragments, and coordinates
   - Encounter dangers: rogue AIs, corrupted creatures, solar storms
   - Manage ship integrity and radiation exposure

4. **RETURN & REBUILD**
   - Bring discoveries back to homebase
   - Process data fragments for G'ejar-Vale coordinates
   - Upgrade systems with salvaged tech
   - Piece together the mystery of The Surge

5. **PROGRESSION**
   - Each run brings you closer to G'ejar-Vale
   - Permanent upgrades unlock new capabilities
   - Narrative fragments reveal the truth
   - The galaxy's secrets slowly unfold

## âš¡ CORE MECHANIC: THE RFE (Reference Frame Engine)

### What Is The RFE?

The **Reference Frame Engine** allows near-instantaneous travel between star systems by collapsing the space-time reference frame between two points. It's the only reason you can survive in this dead galaxyâ€”but it comes at a cost.

### Radiation Fuel System

The RFE requires specific types of stellar radiation to function:

| Star Type | Radiation Type | RFE Fuel Value | Risk Level | Systems Available |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|------------|-------------------|
| **O-Type** (Blue Supergiants) | Extreme UV / X-Ray | Very High | Extreme | Rare |
| **B-Type** (Blue-White) | High UV | High | High | Uncommon |
| **A-Type** (White) | Moderate UV | Medium | Medium | Common |
| **F-Type** (Yellow-White) | Low UV | Low | Low | Common |
| **G-Type** (Yellow - Sun-like) | Balanced | Medium | Low | Common |
| **K-Type** (Orange) | Infrared-Heavy | Low | Very Low | Common |
| **M-Type** (Red Dwarfs) | Deep Infrared | Very Low | Minimal | Very Common |

### Strategic Choices

- **High radiation = more fuel = more jumps** but systems are more dangerous
- **Low radiation = safer** but requires more frequent refueling stops
- **Route planning** becomes critical: balance fuel efficiency vs. danger
- **Degraded stars** post-Surge may emit corrupted radiation (unpredictable effects)

## ðŸ¤– AI CREW SYSTEM

### Fractured Personalities

Your AI crew are not whole. They're reconstructed from salvaged cores, fragmented memory banks, and corrupted code. Each has:

- **Personality quirks** influenced by their damage
- **Specializations** (navigation, combat, research, engineering, etc.)
- **Upgrade paths** as you find missing fragments
- **Unique dialogue** reflecting their fractured nature

### Example AI Crew Members

**ARIA** (Navigation AI)
- Obsessed with precision, paranoid about miscalculations
- "We're off by 0.003%. That's how crews die."
- Upgrade path: Better route optimization, hazard prediction

**FORGE** (Engineering AI)
- Gruff, sarcastic, treats ship like a living thing
- "She's held together by spite and prayers. Don't test her."
- Upgrade path: Faster repairs, jury-rigged solutions

**CIPHER** (Research AI)
- Curious but unstable, occasionally speaks in fragments
- "Data... corrupted... but beautiful. Do you see the pattern?"
- Upgrade path: Better fragment decryption, lore discoveries

**GHOST** (Stealth/Sensors AI)
- Whispers, rarely speaks directly, sees everything
- "...something watching... not alone..."
- Upgrade path: Early threat detection, evasion protocols

## ðŸŒŸ SOLAR SYSTEM EXPLORATION

### System Types

Each solar system contains:

1. **The Star** (radiation source for RFE refuel)
2. **Planetary Bodies** (dead worlds, derelict colonies)
3. **Stations/Outposts** (research facilities, trade hubs, military bases)
4. **Debris Fields** (destroyed ships, salvage opportunities)
5. **Anomalies** (Surge corruption zones, strange signals)

### Points of Interest (POIs)

When exploring a system, you encounter:

- **Abandoned Stations** - Tech salvage, data logs, potential dangers
- **Dead Colonies** - Resources, narrative fragments, environmental hazards
- **Derelict Ships** - Ship upgrades, survivor logs, hostile remnants
- **Research Facilities** - Advanced tech, Surge research, corrupted experiments
- **Signal Sources** - Distress beacons, trap lures, AI transmissions

### Dangers

- **Solar Storms** - Unpredictable radiation bursts
- **Rogue AIs** - Corrupted ship intelligences turned hostile
- **Corrupted Creatures** - Life twisted by Surge radiation
- **Scavenger Crews** - Other survivors, not all friendly
- **Radiation Decay** - Your ship slowly degrades in high-radiation zones
- **The Watchers** - Unknown entities observing your movements

## ðŸŽ¨ UI/UX DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### Terminal-Based Interface

Every screen should feel like:
- A degraded military terminal
- Patched-together salvaged systems
- Holographic overlays barely functioning
- Information you **need**, not want

### Information Hierarchy

1. **Critical** - Hull, fuel, immediate threats (bright cyan, pulsing)
2. **Important** - System status, resources, navigation (cyan glow)
3. **Contextual** - Lore, AI dialogue, flavor (muted white)
4. **Background** - Aesthetic noise, scanlines, flicker

### Atmospheric Elements

- **Glitch text** - Corrupted data occasionally shows
- **AI interjections** - Crew comments appear in feeds
- **Ambient noise** - Terminal hum, static, distant signals
- **Flickering** - Systems aren't stable
- **Radiation warnings** - Surge effects visible on UI

## ðŸ”„ ROGUELIKE ELEMENTS

### Permadeath Lite

- **Ship is permanent** - Upgrades persist
- **AI crew persists** - Relationships and growth remain
- **Expeditions are risky** - You can lose resources and time
- **Critical failures** - Severe setbacks possible but not full reset

### Meta-Progression

- **Homebase upgrades** - Permanent improvements
- **AI reconstruction** - Gradual crew enhancement
- **Knowledge retention** - Discovered lore stays unlocked
- **Route mastery** - Learned paths remain known

### Randomization

- **System layouts** - POIs change each run
- **Loot tables** - Resources vary
- **Encounter types** - Dangers are unpredictable
- **Story fragments** - Discovery order varies
